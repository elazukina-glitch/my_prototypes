10 · iOS onboarding

Data & Friction

Status

Launched comprehensive onboarding data collection

Goal

To understand our incoming user profile to personalize paywalls moving forward and analyze subsequent product behavior (Retention/LTV)

Problem

Noticed a drop in purchase conversion at the onboarding paywall. The root cause was friction created by an excessive number of screens

Iteration 1

Removed 2 screens immediately preceding the paywall. We are closely monitoring the metrics and continuing to iterate on the flow
